Conor's Pizza Recipe

Description

Chef Conor has been a fan of Italian food for as long as he can remember. This love extends to the cooking itself and he has fine tuned his pizza recipe for many years. Here he shares his secret recipe with you

The recipe incorporates several tricks that Conor learned while taking part in a pizza making course in Turin last summer. The result is a fantastically delicious pizza which all are sure to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce of choice
  • 2 cups of fresh sliced mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475F. Prepare all ingredients for the margherita pizza.
  2. On a floured surface, roll the dough into the desired shape. Top it off with pizza sauce, pieces of fresh mozzarella and sprinkle a bit of parmesan cheese.
  3. Bake pizza for about 14 minutes or until the crust is lightly browned and the cheese is golden.
  4. Remove pizza from the oven, top it off with fresh basil. Serve pizza right away, while the cheese is all melted.
